What is TripsPH?

TRIPsPH is a leading provider of innovative payment solutions for public transportation in the Philippines. Our Transportation RFID Integrated Payment System offers a convenient and secure way to pay for your fare, eliminating the need for cash and providing a seamless ticketing experience. With our web and RFID technology, passengers can enjoy mobile POS transactions and real-time data tracking, while bus operators benefit from efficient management reports. TRIPsPH is committed to making public transportation safer, more convenient, and more efficient for everyone.
